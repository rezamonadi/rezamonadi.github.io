I am a Ph.D. student working under the joint supervisions of professor Fred Hamann and professor Simeon Bird. We study quasars in astronomical surveys with machine learning and statistical methods, trying to solve the puzzle of "Galaxy Evolution".

I had the chance of working on pulsars under the supervision of my great supervisor in Iran, professor Seyed Mohad Sadegh Movahed during my M.Sc. program in Shahid Beheshty University (National University of Iran).
