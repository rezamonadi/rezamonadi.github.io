Reza Monadi

I am a PhD student working with Simeon Bird.
We study quasars using astronomical surveys with machine learning/statistical methods, trying to solve the puzzle of Galaxy Evolution .
We are also going to create useful catalogs for other astronomers.

When I am not doing science, I spend my time with my wonderful wife, Asieh.

Projects

Extremely red quasars: We used unsupervised machine learning method to cluster this group of quasars in parameter space and study the properties of their spectra.

Metal lines catalog: With the help of state-of-the-art machine learning techniques such as Gaussian processes we are trying to create useful catalogues using spectroscopic data available in data sets like SDSS.
