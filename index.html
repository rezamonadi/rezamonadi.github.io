I am a PhD student working with Simeon Bird.
We study quasars using astronomical surveys with machine learning/statistical methods, trying to solve the puzzle of Galaxy Evolution.
We are also going to create useful catalogs for other astronomers.

When I am not doing science, I spend my time with my wonderful wife, Asieh.

Projects

Extremely Red Quasars: We used unsupervised machine learning methods to cluster this group of quasars in parameter space and study the properties of their spectra.

Metal Lines Catalog: With the help of state-of-the-art machine learning techniques such as Gaussian processes, we are trying to create useful catalogs using spectroscopic data available in datasets like SDSS.
